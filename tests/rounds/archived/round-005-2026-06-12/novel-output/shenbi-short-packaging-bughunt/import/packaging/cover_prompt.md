## 主 prompt

```
A young man in worn worker's clothes stands at the entrance of a dark rust-colored alleyway, facing away from the viewer, looking toward a massive industrial furnace in the distance that emits a faint blue glow. His posture is tense but steady — one hand rests on the rough stone wall, the other hangs at his side, palm slightly open with faint electric sparks visible around his fingertips. The alley is narrow, framed by crumbling stone and patched iron roofs, with condensation dripping from rusted metal beams above. In the distance beyond the furnace, a dark sky is streaked with purple twilight — the first hint of an air raid's distant fire. On the furnace's corroded metal surface, barely visible, is a faded emblem of a gear with a bird spreading its wings. The composition uses a low-angle three-point perspective with the young man in the center-left third, the furnace anchoring the right third, and the purple skyline creating the horizon. Color palette: rust red + iron gray + industrial blue glow + twilight purple. Style: semi-realistic digital painting with gritty texture, cinematic lighting, Chinese web novel cover aesthetic. Mood: oppressive but with a glimmer of defiance in the electric sparks.
```

